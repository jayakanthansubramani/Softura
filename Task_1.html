honey garlic chicken


Ingrediant's


         ¼ cup honey
        2 tablespoons soy sauce
        ⅛ teaspoon red pepper flakes
        1 ½ tablespoons olive oil
        2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into bite-size piece

Lets Have Some Fun


    Step 1
        Whisk honey, soy sauce, and red pepper flakes in a bowl; set aside.
    Step 2
        Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir chicken in hot oil until lightly brown,
    about 5 minutes.
    Step 3
        Pour honey mixture into the skillet; continue to cook and stir until chicken is no longer pink in the center and sauce is thickened,
    about 5 minutes more.
